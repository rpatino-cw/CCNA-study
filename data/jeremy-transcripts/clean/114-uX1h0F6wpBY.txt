This statement might be a little unclear, but&nbsp;&nbsp;

There are some key benefits to using a split-MAC&nbsp;
architecture. This isn’t a complete list and you&nbsp;&nbsp;
don’t have to memorize it, but you should&nbsp;

WLCs also provide dynamic channel assignment.&nbsp;
The WLC can automatically select which channel&nbsp;&nbsp;

The WLC can also automatically set the appropriate&nbsp;

The WLC can also provide self-healing wireless&nbsp;&nbsp;

the WLC can associate the client with the&nbsp;

the last benefit I’ll mention is security and QoS&nbsp;
management. Central management of security and QoS&nbsp;&nbsp;
policies ensures consistency across the network.&nbsp;
As I said, you don’t have to memorize this list,&nbsp;&nbsp;
but definitely be aware that the split-MAC&nbsp;
architecture offers various benefits.
