simple, but there are two ways to do it. The&nbsp;&nbsp;
first is in interface config mode with the command&nbsp;
SPANNING-TREE PORTFAST. This enables PortFast only&nbsp;&nbsp;

PORTFAST DEFAULT. Instead of enabling PortFast on&nbsp;&nbsp;
an individual port, this enables PortFast&nbsp;

switches. This is because connections between&nbsp;
switches are almost always trunk links, since most&nbsp;&nbsp;
modern LANs use multiple VLANs. But connections&nbsp;
to end hosts are almost always access links,&nbsp;&nbsp;
since most hosts don’t need to send and receive&nbsp;
traffic in multiple VLANs. As we’ll cover in a&nbsp;&nbsp;
minute, it’s very important that you don’t enable&nbsp;
PortFast on ports that connect to other switches.
