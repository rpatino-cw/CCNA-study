WLC Interfaces & Management Access

A Cisco WLC isn't a switch — its "interfaces" are mostly logical, each with one job. Click each interface to see what it does and which one you plug into for first-time setup. Then pick HTTPS / SSH / console for each scenario.

Why interfaces are weird on a WLC

A switch has physical ports each tied to VLANs. A WLC has a few physical ports plus a stack of logical interfaces that map to VLANs. The right interface depends on what you're doing — bootstrapping out of the box (service port), managing it day-to-day (management interface), letting clients land in different VLANs (dynamic interfaces), or absorbing client web-auth redirects (virtual interface). Plug a laptop into the wrong port = silence.
