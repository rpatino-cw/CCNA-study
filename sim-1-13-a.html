How a Switch Decides Where to Send a Framedrill

A switch's whole job: receive a frame, look up the destination MAC in its table, and forward out the matching port. If the dest MAC is not in the table, flood out every port except the one the frame arrived on. This single rule explains 80% of L2 behavior.
