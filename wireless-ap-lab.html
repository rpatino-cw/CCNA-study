Wireless AP Lab — Placement

Drag access points onto the floor plan. Move, clone, delete to design coverage. Phase 1 establishes layout — RF heatmap and configuration arrive in the next builds.

Goal: place enough APs to cover every room without overlapping cells of the same channel. Walls attenuate signal — concrete (−12 dB) much more than drywall (−3 dB) or glass (−2 dB). Each AP serves a coverage cell; in production you size cells for clients-per-AP, not square-footage.

Drag a card onto the plan to place an AP. Drag a placed AP to move it. Right-click for Clone / Delete. Select an AP and press Delete to remove. Layout persists in this browser via localStorage.
