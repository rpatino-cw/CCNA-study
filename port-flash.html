Port & Numbers Flash

Solo timed drill for the rote free points: ports, AD, timers, severity, masks, IPv6.

Single-player spaced-repetition flash drill. Pick a category (hardest first by default) or go mixed. Flip the card and self-grade, or type the answer for instant scoring. Missed cards come back sooner. Three correct in a row masters a card and it drops in frequency.

Facts: CCNA 200-301 v1.1 cheatsheet (ports, administrative distance, OSPF / STP timers, syslog severity, subnet masks, IPv6 prefixes). Solo companion to Port Patrol.
