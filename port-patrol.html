Port Patrol

Well-known ports race. Service ↔ port. First to 4 wins.

Server flips a coin. Either names a service and asks for the port, or shows a port + protocol and asks for the service. First correct gets the round. Best of 7. CCNA exam essentials only — DNS, DHCP, SSH, HTTPS, SNMP, BGP, RADIUS, syslog, and friends.

How to play. Click Start Room, copy the share link, send to a friend. They open the link and drop straight into your room. Match starts when both players are in.

Answers. For port questions, just type the number — 53, 443, 22. Aliases accepted for service questions: SSH, scp, sftp all hit port 22. Capitalization and dashes don't matter.
