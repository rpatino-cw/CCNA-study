APs, WLCs, Antennasdrill

An AP bridges Wi-Fi to wired Ethernet. A WLC centralizes config, channel/power decisions, and roaming for many APs (CAPWAP tunnels carry the data). The antenna shapes where the energy goes — omni for ceilings, patch for walls, Yagi for long shots.
