Multicast, Reserved & Class Range Drill

Five rapid-fire decks built straight from the cheat sheet: IPv6 scope digit, IPv4 well-known multicast, the IPv4↔IPv6 pairs, reserved ranges (plus special IPv4), and Class A/B/C. Pick a deck, answer, repeat until it is reflex.
WHY this exists. These are pure-memory exam points — no math, no subnetting, just recall under time pressure. Cisco loves the pairs: OSPF on .5/ff02::5, EIGRP on .10/ff02::a. Miss the scope digit (ff02 link vs ff0e global) or swap RIP/EIGRP/VRRP and you drop free points. Drill until the address maps to its job instantly.
