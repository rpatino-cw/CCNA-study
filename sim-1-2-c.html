Spine-Leaf — The Data Center Fabricclick to flow

Modern apps are chatty between servers (east-west) — micro-services, distributed databases, GPU training. Three-tier was built for north-south user traffic and gives unpredictable latency between racks. Spine-leaf guarantees every leaf is exactly two hops from every other leaf: leaf → spine → leaf. Always. ECMP load-balances across all spines. Click any leaf as source, then a different leaf as destination.
