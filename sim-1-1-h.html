PoE Budget Mathdrill

Power over Ethernet sounds magical until you realize the switch has a finite power supply. Each PoE class — af, at, bt T3, bt T4 — caps watts per port AND defines how much power the device on the other end actually receives (cable loss eats some). The rule: total PD demand × number-of-ports must NOT exceed the switch PSE budget. Pick a switch class, plug in your devices, see if it fits. The classic exam trap: 24 phones at 25.5W = 612W on a 370W switch — does NOT fit.
