Syslog Severity Sim

Every device produces messages. Some say "the building is on fire" (severity 0). Others say "I considered doing a thing" (severity 7). Eight levels, lower = worse. The router only sends events at-or-below the configured trap level — set it to 4 and you see 0–4, never 5–7.
