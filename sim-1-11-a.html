NSS — Spatial Streamsdrill

A spatial stream is one independent radio signal carrying its own data, riding the same channel by exploiting different physical paths. Add an antenna pair (TX and RX), unlock another stream, multiply throughput. Min(TX,RX) caps NSS — adding a 4th TX with only 2 RX antennas wastes silicon.
