Hub vs Switch — Shared vs Point-to-Pointcollide it

A hub is a dumb repeater: every frame goes to every port, half-duplex, all PCs share one collision domain. Two PCs talking at once = collision = CSMA/CD backoff. A switch learns MAC addresses and forwards each frame only out the right port, full-duplex, every port is its own collision domain. Click "send 4 PCs at once" on each side and watch the difference.
