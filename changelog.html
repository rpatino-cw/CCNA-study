v3.1.0 Previous April 13, 2026
New Games
  • Lab Duels — 1v1 multiplayer competitive labs. Create a duel, share the code, race to complete objectives with real-time opponent tracking.
  • Bug Hunt — 3 troubleshooting labs with pre-broken configs. Diagnose with show commands, find the bugs, fix them. Easy (1 bug), Medium (3), Hard (5).
  • Explain Like I'm Cisco — AI-judged concept explanation game. 33 topics, 60-second timer, scored on Accuracy/Completeness/Clarity/Terminology. Pure recall.
