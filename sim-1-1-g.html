Server Is a Role, Not a Hardware Classdrill

"Server" is a role you play in a transaction, not a permanent identity. Your laptop is a client when you load YouTube. Your laptop becomes a server when your kid AirDrops a photo TO it. The hardware doesn't change. The role does — defined by who initiates the request and who serves the response. Read each scenario, then click the actor that's serving in THIS transaction.
