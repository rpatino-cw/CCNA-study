02 Question Types

Multiple Choice
~60%
Select one correct answer from 4 options. Straightforward knowledge recall and scenario-based reasoning. The bulk of the exam.
Multiple Select
~15%
Select 2-3 correct answers from a list. The question tells you exactly how many to choose. Partial credit is not given.
Performance-Based (PBQs)
~25%
Interactive simulations: drag-and-drop, fill-in-the-blank, matching, and network diagram tasks. Appear at the beginning and end of the exam.
Strategy: Skip PBQs at the start. Complete all multiple choice first, then come back to PBQs with remaining time.
