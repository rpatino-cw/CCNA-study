Three-Tier — Core / Distribution / Accessbuild it

Big campuses have many wiring closets. You can't sanely connect every access switch to every other. The distribution layer aggregates closets and runs Layer-3 to the core. The core just forwards as fast as possible. Each layer has one job. Add access switches to feel the aggregation, then toggle "remove distribution" to watch the design break.
