NTP Stratum Sim

Why NTP exists: every log line, certificate, Kerberos ticket, and DHCP lease is timestamped. Clocks drift seconds per day. Without sync, log correlation is impossible and TLS handshakes fail. NTP organizes the world into a tree — atomic clock at the root, each hop down adds one stratum and a tiny error margin.
