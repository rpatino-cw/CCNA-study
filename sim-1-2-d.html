WAN — Hub-Spoke vs Meshtoggle topology

A WAN connects sites across geography. Cheaper to backhaul everything to HQ (hub-spoke) but the hub becomes the bottleneck and a single failure point. Full mesh is fast and resilient but the link count grows N(N-1)/2. Most enterprises land on partial mesh: critical sites connect directly, the rest funnel through HQ.
