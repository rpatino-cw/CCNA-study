EtherChannel (Deep)

One topic, taught in small beats. Each beat teaches a chunk, then forces you to recall it before you move on. Finish with a blank terminal capstone.

EtherChannel bundles several physical links into ONE logical link. You get more bandwidth and redundancy, and Spanning Tree treats the bundle as a single link so it never blocks your spare cables. Work the beats in order. You cannot advance past a beat until you pass its recall check. The highest-yield idea (which negotiation combos actually form) is drilled the most.
