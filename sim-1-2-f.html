On-Prem vs Cloud — CAPEX vs OPEXslide it

On-prem is buying the building (CAPEX up front, then maintenance). Cloud is renting (OPEX every month, no servers to own). Small/short workloads almost always win in cloud. Big steady workloads at long enough timelines tip back to on-prem. Slide workload size and duration — watch the crossover. Then look at the responsibility stack: each step up (IaaS → PaaS → SaaS) hands more layers to the provider.
