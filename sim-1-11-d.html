Channel Planning — Don't Step on Each Otherdrill

A 2.4 GHz channel is 22 MHz wide but channel centers are only 5 MHz apart — so adjacent channels overlap badly. Only 1, 6, 11 in North America don't overlap. 5 GHz gives you ~25 fully non-overlapping 20 MHz channels — the reason it's the band of choice for dense deployments.

Channel planner

Pick channels for AP-1, AP-2, AP-3. In 2.4 GHz, only 1/6/11 are safe together. Try 1+3+6 — watch the overlap warning fire.
