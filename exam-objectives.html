CCNA vs Network+ — Which first?

Network+ first if you're new to networking. It's vendor-neutral, covers fundamentals, and gives you a foundation. 40 of the 53 CCNA objectives overlap with Network+ content.

CCNA first if you're already working with Cisco gear or targeting a network engineer role. It's deeper, Cisco-specific, and more valued in enterprise environments.

Both? Start with Network+, then do CCNA. 40 of the 53 CCNA objectives overlap with Network+ — that's 75% reuse. Someone who passes Network+ is already 75% of the way to CCNA. This site tracks both with the cert track toggle in Study Group. Your scores carry over when you switch.
