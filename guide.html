Guide

What to click and when.

First time? Do these 3 things.

1 Read the Exam Objectives 5 min. See what the test covers before studying. → 2 Take the Diagnostic 20 min. Shows your weak spots. Don't study first. → 3 Open CORE and study your worst topic Watch the video, then quiz yourself. →

That's day 1. Tomorrow, repeat step 3 on a different topic.

Every day

20-Minute Routine

1Open CORE. Find your weakest topic (red).
2Study it for 15 min (video + reading).
3Take a 10-question quiz on it.
4Done. Come back tomorrow.

Good to know

  • Scores are live. Every quiz, lab, and diagnostic answer updates your score instantly. It weighs recent answers heavier, so improvement shows fast.

  • Answering = score moves. Just reading or exploring doesn't. Simple rule.
