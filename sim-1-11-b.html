2.4 vs 5 GHz — The Tradeoffdrill

Lower frequency travels farther through walls but is crowded (only 3 non-overlapping channels) and slow. Higher frequency carries more bits but can't punch through brick. Real-world planning is choosing which loss you can accept — coverage holes or interference holes.
