Network Management Pickerdrill

Three management approaches, three different sweet spots. Wrong choice = wasted licenses or operator burnout. Read the scenario, pick the approach.

Match the scenario to the approach

Per-device CLI

Small / Simple

SSH into each box. Fast for tiny networks. Falls apart past 20 devices.

NMS polling (SNMP)

Medium / Visibility-first

Central server polls devices, dashboards, alerts. SolarWinds, Zabbix, LibreNMS.

Controller-based (SDN)

Large / Policy-driven

Single controller pushes intent. APIC-EM, DNA Center, ACI.

Pros / cons matrix

ApproachProsCons
Per-device CLIZero infra cost, immediate, full controlDoesn't scale, drift-prone, no visibility
NMS pollingCentralized monitoring, alerts, baselineMostly read-only, polling lag, SNMP weak
Controller-basedPush policy once, drift-proof, abstracts complexityCost, lock-in, learning curve, single-point-failure
