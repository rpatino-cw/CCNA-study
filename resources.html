Strategy Notes

Strategy
The 4-Tool Stack (All You Need)
1. Jeremy's IT Lab (YouTube) — primary course, daily
2. Packet Tracer — CLI every day, no exceptions
3. Anki deck — 15 min/day spaced repetition from day 1
4. Boson ExSim — final 2-3 weeks only

Everything else is supplemental. These four are non-negotiable.
Core stack · $99 total (Boson only)
Strategy
Boson Timing — Don't Buy Early
Buy Boson ExSim 2-3 weeks before your exam date, not before. The $99 hits harder if you waste it early — you won't have enough knowledge to learn from the explanations, and you'll burn through questions you can't retake fresh. Use free practice tests (Easy-Prep, Crucial Exams) during the learning phase. Save Boson for the final push when you can actually gauge readiness.
Timing matters · Buy ~2-3 weeks before exam
Strategy
Subnetting = Free Points
IP Connectivity is 25% of the exam — the largest domain. Subnetting speed is the highest-ROI drill. Practice daily until you can solve any subnet question in under 30 seconds. Use subnettingpractice.com (10 min/day). This is the easiest way to bank guaranteed points.
25% of exam · Drill daily · Target: <30s per question
