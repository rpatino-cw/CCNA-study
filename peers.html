How Your Data Works
This site runs entirely on GitHub Pages — no traditional server, no database, no account system, and no monthly hosting bill. Your study progress lives in two places:
Your Browser
Quiz scores, diagnostic history, streaks, study time — all saved in your browser's localStorage. Instant, private, works offline.
Your Study Group
Every time you study, a full copy of your progress syncs to the group's cloud storage (Cloudflare KV). This is your safety net.
Switch Devices / Recover Your Data
New phone? Reset your computer? No problem.
1. Open this site on any device
2. Go to Study Group → "Recover Your Account"
3. Enter your group code () and your display name
4. Everything restores — quiz history, proficiency scores, streaks, all of it

Why no server? We're experimenting with a peer-to-peer approach where your study group IS your data store. No accounts to create, no passwords to forget, no subscription fees.
