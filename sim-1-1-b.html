Switch Brains: Learning by Watchingdrill

A switch is born blank. It has no idea who lives where. Every frame that arrives teaches it one fact: "the device with this source MAC lives behind this port." That's it. Click a PC to send a frame and watch the table fill itself. Once the table is full, switching is fast — but until then, every unknown destination floods to every port.
