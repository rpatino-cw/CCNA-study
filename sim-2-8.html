WLAN GUI Configuration

A virtual WLC dialog. Pick SSID, security mode, QoS profile, and advanced options. Hit Apply and the SSID goes on the air. The point: each setting maps to a real-world need — voice gets platinum, guest gets bronze, corp gets WPA2-Enterprise.

Why each knob exists

Wi-Fi has three knobs that matter: who can join (security), who gets airtime first (QoS), and how the radio behaves (band, roaming). On a WLC GUI those map directly: General tab (SSID + VLAN + state), Security tab (PSK / Enterprise / WPA3 / Open), QoS tab (Platinum=voice, Gold=video, Silver=data, Bronze=guest), Advanced tab (band-select, fast-roam 802.11r, client-load-balance). Pick wrong and you either lock people out or let everyone in — both are bad.
