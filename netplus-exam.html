Exam Tutorial

Read carefully — this will not count against your time

How This Exam Works

  • 90 questions — a mix of multiple choice and performance-based questions (PBQs)
  • 90 minutes — the timer starts when you click "Begin Exam" and cannot be paused
  • Navigation — use Next/Previous to move between questions, or click any question number in the side panel
  • Flag for Review — mark questions you want to revisit before submitting
  • Answering — click an option to select it. Click again to deselect. Only one answer per question.

Exam Strategy (from CompTIA)

  • PBQs appear first — they take the most time. Consider flagging them and answering multiple-choice questions first, then returning.
  • Unanswered questions are wrong. Never leave a question blank — always guess if you're unsure.
  • Read every word. Pay attention to "MOST likely," "BEST," "FIRST," and "LEAST" — these change the answer.
  • Don't second-guess. Your first instinct is usually correct. Only change an answer if you're certain.
