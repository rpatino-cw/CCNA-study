Single-mode vs Multimode vs Copperslide it

Copper UTP is cheap and easy but runs out of room around 100 m at 1G/10G. Multimode fiber uses an LED light source and a fat core — light bounces along multiple paths (modes), which causes modal dispersion and limits distance. Single-mode uses a laser and a tiny core — one path, one mode, runs 10+ km. Slide distance and bandwidth and watch each medium fail (or hold).
