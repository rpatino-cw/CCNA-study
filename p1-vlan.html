VLANs + Trunking + Inter-VLAN (Deep)

One topic, taught in small beats. Each beat teaches a chunk, then makes you prove it before you move on. End by rebuilding the whole config from a blank terminal.

Objectives 2.1 and 2.2. A VLAN is one broadcast domain. Access ports carry one untagged VLAN to a PC. Trunks carry many VLANs, 802.1Q tagged, between switches. Crossing VLANs needs Layer 3: router on a stick or an L3 switch SVI. Work each beat in order. You cannot advance past a beat until you pass its recall check.
